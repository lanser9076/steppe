The Name

Why Steppe?

Every good product has a story. This one is personal.

The Kazakh steppe is one of the most open landscapes on earth — flat, vast, and completely unobstructed. You can see everything. Nothing is hidden. The horizon is always clear.

That's what we want for your finances. No fog. No surprises. Just a clear view of where your money is, where it's going, and how much ground you have left to cover.

Steppe was built by someone who immigrated from Kazakhstan to Virginia — and whose family gave everything to make sure their son could always know where he stands. This app is built on that same principle.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who want honest budget tracking without handing their financial life to a corporation.

📥
Import Bank Statements
Drop a CSV from SoFi, Chase, or Bank of America. Transactions are auto-categorized and you review before anything saves.
🧠
Learns Your Spending
Assign a category once and the app remembers. Next time that merchant appears, it's already sorted.
🕐
Pending Transactions
Log spending as it happens — before your bank statement arrives. Pending items count toward your budget immediately.
📊
Visual Insights
Monthly spending charts, category breakdowns, cash flow trends, and over-budget alerts — all at a glance.
💰
Income Tracking
Track paychecks and other income. See your real net position — income minus spending — every single month.
🔒
100% Private
Everything lives in your browser's localStorage. No server ever receives your data. Turn on airplane mode — it still works.

Privacy

Your data is yours.
Full stop.

We have no business model that requires reading your finances. Here's exactly how it works.

📱
Stored on your device only
All data lives in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your phone or computer under any circumstances.
🌐
Zero server communication
After the page loads once, no network requests are made. Your transactions never touch a server.
✈️
Airplane mode proof
Turn off WiFi and Steppe still works perfectly. If no internet is needed, no data is being sent.
👁️
Fully open source
Every line of code is public on GitHub. Anyone can verify there's no hidden data collection. No trust required — just read it.

Built by one person.
Kept free on purpose.

Steppe is a side project built and maintained solo. If it helps you feel more in control of your finances, a small tip keeps it alive and improving.
