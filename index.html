Privacy First

Your data stays on your phone

No complicated legal language here. Here's exactly what happens with your data:

📱
It never leaves your device
Everything you enter gets saved in your phone's storage — the same place apps like Notes and Safari save things locally. Nothing is sent anywhere.
✈️
Turn off WiFi — it still works
The app runs completely offline once installed. If it needed to talk to a server, it wouldn't work in airplane mode. It does — so it doesn't.
👁️
The code is public
Anyone can read every line of code on GitHub.
🗂️
You control your backup
Export your data as a file whenever you want. It's yours.
Features

What it does

📥
Import Bank Statements
Drop a CSV from SoFi, Chase, or Bank of America. The app auto-categorizes transactions and you review before anything gets saved.
🧠
Remembers Merchants
Assign a category to a merchant once — next time it shows up in an import, it's already sorted.
🕐
Pending Transactions
Log a purchase before your bank statement arrives. It shows up in your budget right away — and when you import later, the app matches and removes it so nothing is counted twice.
📊
Spending Charts
Monthly breakdown by category, cash flow over time, and a heads-up when you're getting close to a limit.
💰
Income Tracking
Log paychecks and other income. The app shows you the real number — what came in minus what went out.
📤
Backup & Restore
Export your data as a JSON file anytime. If you switch phones or clear your browser, just import it back and pick up where you left off.

Why I built this

I just wanted a working budget app that I could actually use, something that shows me where my money is going without asking me to pay a subscription or hand over my bank login. So I built it for myself, and it works well enough that I figured someone else might find it useful too.

If it helps you, I'm glad. If you want to support it or report something broken, the links below are the best way.

Install

Add it to your home screen

First, tap "Open App" at the top of this page. Once you're inside the app, follow the steps below to install it on your phone so it lives on your home screen like a normal app.

🍎 iPhone · Safari
  • 1Open the app in Safari (not Chrome)
  • 2Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of the screen
  • 3Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • 4Tap Add in the top right
⚠️ Must be Safari — Chrome on iPhone doesn't support this.
🤖 Android · Chrome
  • 1Open the app in Chrome
  • 2Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner
  • 3Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • 4Tap Add
You may see a banner at the bottom of the screen offering to install it automatically.
💻 Desktop · Chrome or Edge
  • 1Open the app in Chrome or Edge
  • 2Look for a small install icon on the right side of the address bar
  • 3Click it and select "Install"
  • 4It opens as its own window, no browser bar
Works fully offline once installed.
